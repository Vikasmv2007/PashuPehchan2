🦬 Indigenous Buffalo Breeds of India

India has 19 recognized buffalo breeds and accounts for over 56% of the world's buffalo population. Buffalo milk is richer in fat and protein than cow milk, making it ideal for dairy products like ghee, paneer, and curd.
