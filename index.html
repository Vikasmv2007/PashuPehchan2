The Challenge

Why Breed Identification Matters

📋

Inaccurate Insurance

Without proper breed identification, livestock insurance valuations are often inaccurate, leading to under-compensation for farmers after loss events.

🧬

Breed Dilution

India's 30+ indigenous cattle breeds face extinction due to indiscriminate crossbreeding, threatening centuries of genetic heritage and local adaptation.

📊

No Centralized Data

The Ministry lacks real-time data on breed distribution across states, making policy planning and resource allocation inefficient.

🌾

Farmer Knowledge Gap

Many smallholder farmers cannot identify the breed of their own animals, leading to suboptimal feeding, breeding, and veterinary care decisions.

Features

Built for the Field

🎯

High-Accuracy CNN Model

Trained on thousands of annotated images of Indian cattle and buffalo, covering 30+ breeds across diverse lighting and field conditions.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Works on low-end smartphones in rural areas. Simple interface requiring no technical expertise from farmers.

🔬

Detailed Breed Reports

Beyond identification — provides breed-specific care tips, vaccination schedules, optimal feed, and productivity benchmarks.

💰

Economic Valuation

Estimated market value helps farmers negotiate fair prices and enables accurate insurance premium calculation for officials.

🗺️

Geo-tagged Records

Location data with each submission creates a real-time national map of breed distribution for government planning.

🛡️

Offline Resilience

Designed to work with low connectivity — image processing queued and synced when connection is restored.
