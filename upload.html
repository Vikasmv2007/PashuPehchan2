📌 Tips for Best Results

  • ☀️
    Good Lighting
    Photograph in natural daylight. Avoid harsh shadows or backlight.
  • 📐
    Clear Angle
    A side-profile or 3/4-front view captures key morphological features like horns, hump, and ear shape.
  • 📏
    Full Body Frame
    Include the full body of the animal — head to tail — in the frame for maximum accuracy.
  • 🎯
    Single Animal
    One animal per photo gives the AI the best chance to analyse individual traits accurately.
  • 🚫
    No Obstructions
    Avoid photos where ropes, yokes, or fences partially cover the animal.
